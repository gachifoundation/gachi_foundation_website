Donation & Refund Policy

{{ ngo.name }} is grateful for every contribution. This policy explains how donations are handled.

How donations are used

All donations support our programs in education, environment and livelihoods across Dumka, Pakur, Godda and Sahibganj, Jharkhand.

Receipts & tax exemption

A receipt is issued for every donation. Tax-exemption certificates will be provided where applicable as registrations are completed.

Refunds

Donations are generally non-refundable. If a donation was made in error or an incorrect amount was charged, contact us within 7 days at {{ ngo.email }} and we will review the request in good faith.

Security

We do not store card or bank credentials on this website. Payments are made directly via your bank or UPI app using the details on our Bank Details page.
